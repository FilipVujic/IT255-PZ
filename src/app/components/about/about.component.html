Vujic Music Shop is a Serbian-based retailer of musical instruments, studio, lighting, and pro-audio equipment. VMS became widely known primarily due to its large online retail operation, VMS Cyberstore. According to a 2014 articles in the largest Balkan newspaper based in Belgrade, the CDC, VMS is the largest online retailer of its category of merchandise, worldwide.


Filip Vujic Sr., founded the company sixty-six years ago (in 1954) as a family business in Belgrade — Serbia's capital city — where the Vujic Music Shop headquarters endures today (2020). And, as of 2020, the company is still family-owned.
